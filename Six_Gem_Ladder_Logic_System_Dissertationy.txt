ABSTRACT
=============================================================================
Tier 1 of the 6-Gem system established the Stream Inference operator [A, B, C] as 
a ternary-first phase collapse over a hexagonal manifold of six discrete states. 
Tier 2.1 extends this foundation into a scalable, high-order architecture known

isolated calculations to continuous logical streams. This framework incorporates 
Negative Logic Auditors (-6g) for error correction, Modular Carriage Calculus

in a 167-rung simulator capable of processing 1,002-gem logic streams with 
deterministic replayability and full history archiving.

=============================================================================
I. THE CLASSICAL BRIDGE (DE-TERNARIZATION)
=============================================================================
To ensure downward compatibility with binary logic, classical operators are 
mapped onto fixed-witness streams within the Z6 manifold:

- NEGATION (¬A): (A + 3) mod 6. 
  Provides a perfect 180-degree phase flip (L0 <-> L3, L1 <-> L4).
- CONJUNCTION (A ^ B): [A, B, 0]. 
  Utilizes the Affirmative Witness (L0) to ground the intersection.
- DISJUNCTION (A v B): [A, B, 1]. 
  Utilizes the Potential Flux witness (L1) to introduce affirmative bias.

=============================================================================
II. THE PADDED LADDER ARCHITECTURE
=============================================================================
A "Ladder" is a recursive data structure where the output of Rung(n) defines 
the context for Rung(n+1). 
- SCALABILITY: Implemented from 3-rung "Short Ladders" up to 167-rung 
  "Massive Ladders" (1,002 Gems).
- LOGIC AUDITORS: The introduction of Negative Carriages (-6g) allows for 
  active phase-subtraction, serving as a logical "noise-canceling" mechanism 
  to counteract cumulative drift in deep inference chains.

=============================================================================
III. PARACONSISTENCY & EXPLOSION RESISTANCE
=============================================================================
In classical logic, a contradiction (P ^ ¬P) triggers the Principle of 
Explosion (Ex Falso Quodlibet). In 6-Gem Logic, contradictions (L0 + L3) 
collapse into stable states of "High Dissonance" (L2/L5). 
- RESULTS: 500+ trial audits confirm zero system breakdowns. Contradiction is 
  treated as "Informational Tension" rather than a terminal error, making 
  the system natively Paraconsistent.

=============================================================================
IV. MODAL OPERATORS & QUANTIFIERS
=============================================================================
The system replaces standard symbols with "Gem" equivalents to ensure 
cross-platform clarity:
- NECESSITY (◈A): Holds if [A, 0, w] never yields L3 (Negation) across 
  all 6 possible witnesses.
- POSSIBILITY (◇A): Holds if there exists at least one witness w that 
  collapses the stream to L0 (Affirmation).
- QUANTIFIERS: Universal (∀) is defined as "Ladder Solidity" (all rungs 
  non-negative); Existential (∃) is defined as "State Emergence" (at least 
  one rung collapses to L0).

=============================================================================

- EXPRESSION EVALUATOR: Supports complex strings (e.g., 6g + 6g * 6g - 6g)
